Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

MathShortsUploader does not collect, store, or share any personal data from visitors or third parties.

  • This tool only accesses the social media account(s) directly owned and authorized by the operator, via official OAuth authentication provided by each platform.
  • No data belonging to other users is accessed, processed, or stored.
  • No analytics, tracking cookies, or third-party advertising scripts are used on this website.
  • Video content is generated locally by the operator and published directly to the operator's own authorized accounts.
  • Authentication tokens used to publish content are stored securely and only on the operator's own device.

For privacy questions, contact the operator via the social media accounts associated with this project.
